## Areas

### Village
- **Theme:** Safe zone — no enemies
- **NPCs:** Villager, Elder Hiroshi, Princess Yuki
- **Stations:** Fishing spot, Cooking fire
- **Exits:** North to Dark Forest, East to Haunted Shrine

### Dark Forest
- **Theme:** Dense woodland with green tint
- **Enemies:** 2 Skulls, 2 Spirits
- **Stations:** Fishing spot
- **Resources:** 9 Trees
- **Exits:** South to Village

### Haunted Shrine
- **Theme:** Eerie ruins with purple tint
- **Enemies:** 2 Skulls, 3 Spirits
- **Stations:** 3 Mining rocks, Smithing anvil
- **Exits:** West to Village

## Skills

All skills use the RuneScape XP formula with a max level of 99.

### Combat
- **How:** Kill enemies — XP awarded on kill
- **Stat growth per level:** +12 HP, +3 ATK, +2 DEF, +1 SPD

### Woodcutting
- **How:** Attack trees with Space — 5 hits to fell
- **XP:** 8 per chop
- **Reward:** Wood (amount scales with level: 1 + floor(level/3))
- **Trees respawn** after 12 seconds

### Fishing
- **How:** Press E near a water ripple fishing spot
- **Cast time:** ~2 seconds
- **Catches by level:**

| Fish | Level Req | XP | Weight |
|------|-----------|-----|--------|
| Raw Shrimp | 1 | 10 | 40 |
| Raw Fish | 5 | 18 | 35 |
| Raw Octopus | 10 | 30 | 20 |
| Raw Calamari | 20 | 50 | 5 |

### Mining
- **How:** Attack rocks with Space — 4 hits to deplete
- **XP:** 5 per hit + bonus XP when ore is received
- **Rocks respawn** after 15 seconds
- **Ores by level:**

| Ore | Level Req | XP | Weight |
|-----|-----------|-----|--------|
| Copper Ore | 1 | 10 | 50 |
| Iron Ore | 10 | 20 | 30 |
| Silver Ore | 20 | 35 | 15 |
| Gold Ore | 30 | 55 | 5 |

### Cooking
- **How:** Press E near a cooking fire (permanent or player-lit)
- **Cooks** the first eligible raw item in your inventory
- **Recipes:**

| Raw Item | Result | Level Req | XP | Heals |
|----------|--------|-----------|-----|-------|
| Raw Shrimp | Cooked Shrimp | 1 | 12 | 15 HP |
| Raw Fish | Cooked Fish | 5 | 22 | 30 HP |
| Raw Octopus | Cooked Octopus | 12 | 40 | 50 HP |
| Raw Calamari | Cooked Calamari | 25 | 65 | 80 HP |

### Smithing
- **How:** Press E near an anvil with ore in inventory
- **Crafts** the first eligible recipe
- **Recipes:**

| Recipe | Ore Cost | Level Req | XP | Result |
|--------|----------|-----------|-----|--------|
| Copper Sword | 2 Copper | 1 | 20 | +3 ATK weapon |
| Copper Armor | 3 Copper | 5 | 30 | +4 DEF armor |
| Iron Sword | 2 Iron | 10 | 40 | +8 ATK weapon |
| Iron Armor | 3 Iron | 15 | 55 | +8 DEF armor |
| Silver Sword | 2 Silver | 20 | 55 | +16 ATK weapon |
| Silver Ring | 2 Silver | 20 | 50 | +3 ATK/DEF accessory |
| Silver Armor | 3 Silver | 25 | 65 | +12 DEF, +15 HP armor |
| Magic Wand | 2 Gold | 30 | 85 | +20 ATK weapon |
| Gold Necklace | 2 Gold | 30 | 80 | +5 ATK/DEF, +30 HP accessory |
| Gold Armor | 3 Gold | 35 | 100 | +16 DEF, +30 HP armor |

### Firemaking
- **How:** Press F anywhere with wood in inventory
- **XP:** 15 per fire lit
- **Creates** a temporary cooking fire at your feet
- **Duration:** 30 seconds + 1 second per Firemaking level
- Fire fades away when it burns out

### Thieving
- **How:** Press T near an NPC to pickpocket
- **Cooldown:** 3 seconds between attempts
- **Success rate:** 50% at level 1, scaling to 95% at level 50+
- **Success:** Steal an item + 15 XP
- **Failure:** Take 5 damage + 3 XP
- **Possible loot:** Gold Coin, Silver Coin, Red Gem, Life Potion

## Mechanics

### Combat
- **Attack:** Press Space to slash in your facing direction
- **Hit radius:** 20px from slash point
- **Contact damage:** Enemies deal damage on touch
- **Invincibility:** 900ms after taking damage (player blinks)
- **Knockback:** Applied to both player and enemies on hit

### Leveling
- Uses the RuneScape XP formula: `XP(L) = floor(1/4 * sum(l=1..L-1, floor(l + 300 * 2^(l/7))))`
- Max level: 99 for all skills
- Combat level-up fully heals the player

### Equipment
- 3 slots: Weapon (WPN), Armor (ARM), Accessory (ACC)
- Click equippable items in inventory to equip
- Click equipped items to unequip
- Stats from equipment stack with level-based stats

### Saving
- **Auto-save:** Triggers on every area transition
- **Manual save:** Press S
- **Auto-load:** Game restores on page refresh
- **Death:** Clears save — full reset to Village at level 1

### Death
- All progress is lost (level, skills, inventory, equipment)
- Respawn in Village at level 1
